Key Features

Prime Video

Access to Prime Video, which includes a vast library of movies, TV shows, and Amazon Originals. Members can stream or download content for offline viewing.

Prime Music

Prime members have access to a selection of ad-free music streaming, with the ability to create and curate playlists. Amazon Music Unlimited, a premium music service, is also available at an additional cost.

Amazon Family

Access to Amazon Family benefits, including 20% off diaper subscriptions and exclusive family-oriented offers and discounts.
